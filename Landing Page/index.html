About Us

Welcome to Naksha Aranya – a digital platform committed to the preservation, protection, and promotion of wildlife and natural ecosystems. At the heart of our mission is a deep respect for all living beings and a belief that human progress should never come at the cost of nature's beauty and biodiversity.

Our work spans four key pillars: Wildlife Tourism that is ethical and educative, Animal Adoption programs to support conservation from home, Rescue and Rehabilitation for endangered or injured animals, and Re-homing Services to provide safe havens for animals in distress.

Through immersive video stories, data-driven maps, and engaging user experiences, Naksha Aranya aims to bridge the gap between humans and the wild. Whether you’re a traveler, a student, a wildlife lover, or a conservationist, our platform empowers you to take meaningful action for the environment.

Join us in rewriting the story of coexistence. One act of kindness, one adopted animal, one rescued soul at a time.
