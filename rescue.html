About Our Rescue Center

Our mission is to rescue, rehabilitate, and release wild animals back to their natural habitat. Our team of dedicated wildlife experts and veterinarians ensure each animal gets the care they need.

Funding Through Eco-Tourism

Your visit plays a vital role in supporting our wildlife rescue operations. Revenue from eco-tourism directly funds animal rescues, medical treatments, and habitat preservation efforts. Every tour contributes to food, shelter, and rehabilitation for rescued animals while also creating employment for local communities and raising awareness about conservation.
